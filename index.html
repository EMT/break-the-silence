Break the Silence is an experimental project seeking to engage the creative industries in climate change.

We’re asking what does climate change mean to you?

Is it something you’re talking about at work?

Are you concerned by the lack of action?

Do you ever think about the role the creative industries could play in the climate change story?

We’re encouraging people to speak up with their thoughts, feelings and opinions.

To date we’ve interviewed a number of creative professionals, as well as climate scientists and cultural commentators. A small group have also started to explore early intervention ideas.

Here you can watch the trailer of a film we’re making and browse some of these early ideas.

If you’ve got something to say on any of this, please speak up and add your voice.

And sign up if you’d like to see the finished film and stay involved with the project.
