Break the Silence is an experimental project seeking to engage the creative industries in climate change.

We’re asking what does climate change mean to you? Is it something you’re talking about at work, exploring with clients and agencies? Are you concerned by the lack of action? Do you ever think about the role the creative industries could play in the climate change story?

We’re encouraging people to speak up on their own thoughts, feelings and opinions.

To date we’ve interviewed a number of creative professionals, as well as climate scientists and cultural commentators. A small group have also started to explore early intervention ideas. Here you can find the trailer of a longer film of interviews we will be releasing as well as some emerging ideas.

If you’ve got something to say on this please do speak up and add your voice and thoughts right here. And sign up here if you’d like to see the finished film and stay involved with the next phase of the project.
